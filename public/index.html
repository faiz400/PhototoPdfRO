RO Audit Photo Collection

Pick the option that fits the site and the phone you're using. All three use the same 34-item checklist and produce the same kind of PDF report.

Option 1 · No-Upload Mode

Works fully offline in the browser

Nothing is uploaded anywhere — photos are processed and the PDF is built right in this browser tab, then downloaded directly. Immune to network/server issues since there's nothing to upload or wait on.

Safest option overall. Best suited to newer phones with heavier photo files (lots of CPU/RAM headroom to process them locally) and to sites with network issues.

Use No-Upload Mode →
Option 2 · Online Mode

Server does the heavy lifting

Photos are uploaded and the PDF is generated on the server instead of on your phone — lighter on the device, but every step depends on a steady connection.

Best for low-end devices that may struggle to process lots of high-resolution photos themselves. Requires good, stable internet throughout.

Use Online Mode →
Option 3 · Android App

Standalone installable app

A separate Android app (.apk) that works completely offline, with its own camera capture and PDF generation built in — no browser involved at all.

Best for low-end processors/cameras combined with poor internet — the native app handles weaker hardware more gracefully than a browser tab, and needs no connection during the audit at all.
